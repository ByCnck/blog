百度的url里汉字编码方式，a标签mailto里汉字乱码解决办法

gb2312编码(IE里调用vbscript，非ie使用编码库查询):



Download this demo

In .net,you can only use:
System.Web.HttpUtility.UrlEncode(url, Encoding.GetEncoding("gb2312"))
or In java,you can also:
java.net.URLDecoder.decode("%B0%D9%B6%C8%D6%D0%CE%C4%B1%E0%C2%EB","gb2312")

If your page is gbk, there is another good way.
